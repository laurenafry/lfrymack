How it works

From idea to finished piece

Every commission follows a simple, collaborative process.

1
Reach Out

Tell me what you're imagining — even if it's just a feeling or a rough idea. I love a good challenge.

2
Consult

We chat about medium, size, timeline, and budget. I'll suggest approaches you might not have considered.

3
Create

I get to work. For larger pieces I'll share progress updates and welcome your input along the way.

4
Deliver

Your finished piece arrives ready to enjoy — packaged with care, with full satisfaction guaranteed.
