About the Artist

Where science
meets craft.

My art begins outdoors — in the field, in the forest, at the water's edge. Growing up I was always outside, searching for animals, fishing, climbing way too high up in a tree, ever in search of the perfect stick. Studying wildlife and conservation biology at Colorado State University gave me an intimate understanding of the natural world: the way light hits a feather, how lichen grows across stone, the improbable colors of a Colorado meadow.

That knowledge is the foundation of everything I make. Whether I'm painting a miniature portrait in watercolor or sculpting a tiny creature from clay, nature is always the starting point.

I am lucky enough to call Colorado home, so you may recognize some landscapes from along the front range, or the Vail valley. My home away from home will always be in Scotland. Having married a true scotsman, if we aren't with family, we love spending time in the highlands and exploring every inch of the gorgeous country!

I take commissions across a wide range of mediums and love collaborating with people who want something truly one-of-a-kind.

Signature Service

Blended
Covers

Outlets and switch plates are the last thing most people think about — which is exactly why they stand out. A bright white plate against a hand-laid stone backsplash or a dramatic wallpapered wall can undo an entire design scheme.

I paint each outlet or switch plate cover by hand to seamlessly match your surface — tile, stone, wallpaper, wood, plaster, or paint — so the hardware simply disappears into your design.

Custom painted outlet covers, switch plates, and panel covers for kitchens, bathrooms, living spaces, and anywhere else you want the beauty to go uninterrupted.
